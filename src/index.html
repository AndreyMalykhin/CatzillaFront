For those who tired of cute games,
Good Cat Gone Bad is an endless running game for Android, featuring badass cat
that will smash things and avoid getting killed.

Features:

  • This running game is free to play forever. Play it as long as you like, anytime and anywhere you like it without spending a dime.
  • Guide the giant badass cat that destroys everything in its path.
  • Unique control and gameplay.
  • Smash houses, cars and pedestrians trying to escape from you. Show how evil you are and earn scores.
  • Avoid cops shooting at you and landmines, which will blow you up.
  • Play as long as you still have lives. Things will get progressively harder though, so watch out.
  • This is a game for Android, exclusively.

Will be available on Google Play™ from 4th May 2016
